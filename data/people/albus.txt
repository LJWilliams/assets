Prof. Albus Dumbledore (BA, Mathematics and Computer Science,
Cambridge, 1971; MA, Mathematics, Cambridge, 1973; PhD, Mathematics,
Cambridge, 1977) has made many contributions to the study of large
random graphs (particularly those that do not satisfy Snape's
Inequality).  A former chair of graduate studies in the Department of
Mathematics at Euphoric State University, he is a two-time winner of
the Norbert Wiener Memorial Prize for Advanced Forgetfulness.
